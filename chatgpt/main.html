About Me

Im Chassidy. a passionate web designer and front-end tinkerer from the Netherlands. Art and design are at the heart of everything I do theyre what keep me inspired and drive me forward. I see life through an artistic lens, and I love creating websites that are alive, playful, and meaningful.

What started as a school project quickly became a true passion. I enjoy experimenting with modern, minimal design, smooth animations, and interactive experiences that make every website unique.

When Im not coding, youll probably find me outside, sketching new ideas, exploring visual styles, or finding ways to make websites feel a little more human.
